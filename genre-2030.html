Genre 2030

This was one of my favorite projects. Charlie Warner, the man behind the vision, came to me with a great idea of marketing to millenials; for Madison Gas and Electric. Charlie wanted to try to teach this younger generation about energy in a way that would interest them. He created short films with subtle product placement to do this, but needed it to be branded for many media avenues, as well as a website to accompany and display the videos.

A lot of research was conducted around the target audience, from color schemes they prefer, how they consume content and more. Knowing this project was short film focused, we also wanted the logo to be representative of that. The logotype has the vintage movie theater sinage look; while the icon has many elements, including an iris of a camera.

Once the branding was close to complete and the content was being developed, I began wireframing the website layout. A few ideas were tossed around, but we eventually landed on a minimalist layout. Large images with buttons to access content keeps the focus on the images and videos.
