Energy 2030 Together

This project for Madison Gas and Electric is a big change in direction. In both corporate initiatives and how they share information. This project had a very fast timeline to create the branding and website. Initially, another design firm was brought in, but my design and concept was chosen over it. The idea behind the project was to create a brand that people could trust and rely on. MGE wanted a website that could share energy related articles with the general public.

I always like to add little things for poeple to find in logos, and this one is no exception. The e is a leaf, while the negative space is also a seed. I thought this was appropriate since this project was just starting. The color scheme is bright, energetic; a change from all other MGE related websites.

Knowing this site would be continually adding articles, we kept the layout simple, and easy for a user to navigate. Larger pictures and videos are used to assist in conveying the businesses message. The wide color scheme is used in the tagging of articles. We tested and tweaked the site with users before launch, and the business is very happy with the product. Development and feature enhancement continues, as we are constantly improving the site.
